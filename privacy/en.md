# Flowdi Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

Flowdi ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app collects, uses, and safeguards your information. **We do not collect or store any personal information (such as name, email, or phone number) on our servers.** All your personal data (To-Dos, Schedules, Categories) is stored locally on your device.

## 2. Information Collection and Use

### A. Local Data

Your task and schedule data is stored securely **within your device** using valid local storage methods. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly choose to backup or export it (e.g., via email).

### B. Analytics (Anonymous)

We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service quality.

- **Collected Items:** App version, device platform (iOS/Android), screen views, and anonymous interaction events.
- **Method:** Data is sent to a secure Google Apps Script endpoint.
- **Personal Identification:** The data is associated with a random anonymous user ID or device hash. It cannot be used to identify you personally.

### C. Advertising (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use the following information to provide personalized ads:

- **Collected Items:** Device information, Advertising Identifier (IDFA/ADID), usage data.
- **Third-Party Service:** Google LLC.
- **Opt-out:** You can control personalized ads tracking via your device settings (Settings > Privacy > Tracking on iOS).
- For more details, please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).

## 3. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
